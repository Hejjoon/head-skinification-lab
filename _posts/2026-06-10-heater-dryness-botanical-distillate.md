You sit in your heated office in the middle of winter. The warm air blows continuously, and your skin feels dry and parched. Soon, a tight, pulling sensation spreads across your crown, making your scalp feel stiff and sensitized. You reach up to touch it, feeling a dry, rough texture at your parting.

To soothe this dry feeling, many people apply heavy, oil-based hair creams or rich serums directly to their roots. They hope to lock in moisture. This is a counterproductive reaction. Heavy oils can clog the delicate hair follicles and trap old sebum, causing follicular congestion and micro-inflammation. Furthermore, if symptoms persist or worsen, consulting a dermatologist is highly recommended.

The scalp is a breathing biological barrier that needs lightweight hydration, not heavy, suffocating blockages. It requires clean, aqueous moisture to restore its natural stratum corneum thickness.

Botanical distillates, such as cypress water (*Chamaecyparis obtusa* distillate), provide an excellent solution. They deliver lightweight hydration that matches the scalp's natural moisture needs. Spraying a fine mist of pure plant water onto a dry scalp helps lock in moisture, calm the pulling sensation, and soothe the barrier without heavy residue.
