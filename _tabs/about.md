## Welcome to Head Skinification Lab

I am **Scalp Bio-Hacker Choi**, an independent researcher and heavy user dedicated to exploring scalp barrier biology. 

This lab is a commercial-free, educational space. My mission is to dissect the biological mechanisms of the scalp skin, track consumer concerns, and provide evidence-based, non-medical daily routines.

### Core Philosophy
- **Scalp is Skin**: Treating the head skin with the same rigor, hydration, and respect as facial skin.
- **Evidence Over Marketing**: Shunning corporate sales jargon and legacy brand influence to focus on raw biochemistry (TEWL, stratum corneum, pH homeostasis).
- **Daily Protective Habits**: Empowering you with minimal, science-backed routines to balance sebum and restore comfort.

> **Disclaimer**: The findings shared here represent independent research and personal observations. If symptoms persist or worsen, consulting a dermatologist is highly recommended.
